Site Purpose

The Lagos state Ikoyi Chamber of Commerce and Industry was established in the yeat 1888 for the promotion and protection of Trade and Industry and to represent and express the opinion of the business community on matters affecting trade and industry in the city of Lagos, the Lagos Port Complex, Lagos State and where applicable, the whole Federation. It is a non-profit making organisation and its income and property are applied solely for the promotion of its objectives. Further more the Ikoyi Chamber of commerce its aim To develop and grow an engaged membership and protect the interests of the Organised Private Sector through good governance, advocacy, enabling business opportunities, and providing business support services.
